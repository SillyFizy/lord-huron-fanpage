Discover the history, influences, and secrets behind Lord Huron’s songs and albums

Lord Huron is an American indie rock band based in Los Angeles, founded by Ben Schneider in 2010. The band is known for their cinematic and atmospheric sound, inspired by nature, folklore, and literature. Their albums are like musical novels, full of characters, plots, and mysteries1
